Nuclear

Nuclear power has one of the lowest levels of fatalities per unit of energy generated compared to other energy sources.


Most countries in the world do not use nuclear to produce electricity. France has the largest proportion of nuclear power generation, and it has a large gap with other countries. Japan suddenly reduce its nuclear power generation proportion since 2011, probably because of the earthquake in March 2011.
